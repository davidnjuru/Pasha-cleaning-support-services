About Us

PASHA Cleaning, Care and Support Services LTD is a UK-based company dedicated to delivering exceptional domestic and care services. We focus on professionalism, compassion, and reliability in all our work — whether we’re cleaning a home or supporting a client with daily care needs.

Our Services

Domestic Cleaning

  • General Housekeeping (dusting, vacuuming, surface cleaning)
  • Deep Kitchen and Bathroom Cleaning
  • Window and Glass Polishing
  • Oven, Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning
  • Laundry, Ironing and Folding Services
  • Spring Cleaning and One-off Projects

Care & Support Services

  • Assisted Living Support for Elderly and Disabled Clients
  • Companionship and Wellness Visits
  • Meal Preparation and Medication Reminders
  • Assistance with Dressing, Hygiene & Mobility
  • Light Housekeeping & Errand Running

Commercial Cleaning

  • Office Cleaning Contracts
  • Retail Store Cleaning
  • End-of-Tenancy and Move-Out Cleaning
  • Care Home and Medical Facility Cleaning
